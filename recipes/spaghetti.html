Spaghetti

Spaghetti is an Italian favorite dish that is simple to make while also being delicious. This recipe will be incredibly easy to follow and fast to make. After acquiring the ingredients (listed below), you should be able to craft the dish utilizing our instructions without much effort. Just make sure that during your cooking process you pay close attention to all pans which will remain on the stove. Keep in mind it is recommended before starting this recipe that you have the basic understanding of how to work with stove and other kitchen appliances.

List of ingredients:

    2 Tablespoons diced garlic
    3 Tablespoons Parsley
    1 Pound dried spaghetti
    2 Ragu tomatoe sauce (45 ounces)
    1-2 Cans diced tomatoes (any added flavor)
    1 Large green bell pepper
    3 Teaspoons salt
    2 Teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
    1 Tablespoon hotsauce
    1 Medium yellow onion
    2 Tablespoon oregano
    1/2 Cup water

Steps:

    Set a pan of water on high heated stove
    Dump both ragu sauce into a seperate pan and place on low heated stove
    Slice the yellow onion into small dices and put in sauce
    Place your bell pepper into a blender for 10 seconds then put add it to your sauce
    Add your 2 canned tamatoes into the sauce
    Check your water and see if it's boiling, if so grab your spaghetti and place in boiling water. If not then proceed with the next step and wait for water to boil
    Gently stir your spaghetti for 30 seconds after placing in boiling water
    Add 1/2 water to sauce
    Add hotsauce to sauce
    Add diced garlic to sauce
    Add all the spices inside sauce
    stir you sauce, make sure to repeat this every 3 minutes while spagetthi is cooking
    Once your spaghetti is soft and well textured, use a strainer to strain the spaghetti
    Lastly check to see if sauce is well hot, if so this makes for you final step!
