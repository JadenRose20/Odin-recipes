Spaghetti

Spaghetti is an Italian favorite dish that is simple to make while also being delicious. This recipe will be incredibly easy to follow and fast to make. After acquiring the ingredients (listed below), you should be able to craft the dish utilizing our instructions without much effort. Just make sure that during your cooking process you pay close attention to all pans which will remain on the stove. Keep in mind it is recommended before starting this recipe that you have the basic understanding of how to work with a stove, as well as other kitchen appliances.

List of ingredients:

  • 2 Tablespoons diced garlic
  • 3 Tablespoons Parsley
  • 1 Pound dried spaghetti
  • 2 Ragu tomatoe sauce (45 ounces)
  • 1-2 Cans diced tomatoes (any added flavor)
  • 1 Large green bell pepper
  • 3 Teaspoons salt
  • 2 Teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon hotsauce
  • 1 Medium yellow onion
  • 2 Tablespoon oregano
  • 1/2 Cup water

Steps:

  1. Set a pan of water on high heated stove
  2. Dump both ragu sauce into a seperate pan and place on low heated stove
  3. Slice the yellow onion into small dices and put in sauce
  4. Place your bell pepper into a blender for 10 seconds then put add it to your sauce
  5. Add your 2 canned tamatoes into the sauce
  6. Check your water and see if it's boiling, if so grab your spaghetti and place in boiling water. If not then proceed with the next step and wait for water to boil
  7. Gently stir your spaghetti for 30 seconds after placing in boiling water
  8. Add 1/2 water to sauce
  9. Add hotsauce to sauce
  10. Add diced garlic to sauce
  11. Add all the spices inside sauce
  12. stir you sauce, make sure to repeat this every 3 minutes while spagetthi is cooking
  13. Once your spaghetti is soft and well textured, use a strainer to strain the spaghetti
  14. Lastly check to see if sauce is well hot, if so this makes for you final step!
